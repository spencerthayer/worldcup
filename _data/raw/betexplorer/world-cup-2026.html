What is BetExplorer?

BetExplorer is a place where you can find comprehensive statistics for many of the football leagues and tournaments worldwide, as well as basketball and several other sports. The platform provides you with both statistics and odds comparison from leading bookmakers in your regions for numerous match markets.

The football stats available can be utilised both pre-match and in-play to ensure you are placing bets with all the necessary data at your disposal. But the detailed nature of the statistics can be found for other sports too, ensuring that the platform is the go-to place for sports statistics and results.

You can find a detailed overview of form, head-to-head results, player performance, and even live scores if you regularly bet on live matches. These precise statistics can help elevate your betting experience on a number of different markets, while also ensuring you get the best odds from leading bookmakers thanks to the odds comparison tool.

In addition to a multitude of sports statistics, you can also find popular bets to figure out what other people are betting on or finding value in, odds movements are spotted and provided, and you can take advantage of impressive sports betting offers with bookmakers.

Team and Player Streaks

Track the longest streaks across all the sports we cover, including teams on winning streaks, drawing, or losing streaks. You can also explore trends such as over/under performance, best offence, and top defences. This feature helps you identify patterns that could influence your next bet.

Popular Bets

Discover which matches are attracting the most attention from other bettors with popular bets. Whether it's football, basketball, tennis, or hockey, see where the action is happening across all sports. Use this insight to guide your betting strategy by following popular trends.

Mobile-Friendly

They help us to know which pages are the most and least popular and see how visitors move around the site.  If you do not allow these cookies we will not know when you have visited our site, and will not be able to monitor and improve its performance.

Targeting Cookies

These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used by those companies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant adverts on other sites. They do not store directly personal information, but are based on uniquely identifying your browser and internet device. If you do not allow these cookies, you will experience less targeted advertising.

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalisation. They may be set by us or by third party providers whose services we have added to our pages.  If you do not allow these cookies then some or all of these services may not function properly.
